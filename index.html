Our Story

Biryani Mahal is the biggest chain of biryani-shop in Kolkata.It’s the perfect partnership of outstanding hospitality and exquisite food in an iconic setting in Kolkata City that makes Biryani Mahal one of The World’s Best Restaurants. Offering food that is robust, honest and full of flavour, Biryani Mahal showcases Mughlai cuisine with Indian influences. Subtly reinventing classic favourites, such as handmade biryani’s and great cuts of meat, Biryani Mahal is dedicated to using the finest, freshest ingredients and to serving delicious food.
